Not-So-Short URL Shortener

Works like a normal URL shortener but in many cases creates longer URLs :(

Note: does not support URLs with anchors (#)

Usage:

https://excited-margin.glitch.me/new/https://google.com/

Response:

{"originalUrl":"https://google.com/", "shortUrl":"https://excited-margin.glitch.me/1000"}

Now visit https://excited-margin.glitch.me/1000 to be redirected to https://google.com/
